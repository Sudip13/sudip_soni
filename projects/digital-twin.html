Real-time Digital Twin

RoleLead XR Developer — Architecture & Optimization
TechUnity, Digital Twin Architecture, Blender, Python

Description

Interactive digital twin for industrial equipment with live telemetry, synchronized 3D visualization, and training scenarios. Built to provide engineers with real-time interactive visualization that synchronizes telemetry, simulates failure scenarios, and provides guided training for maintenance and operator onboarding.

Outcome

Reduced onboarding time by 35% in internal pilots with real-time visualization across web and desktop clients. Implemented delta compression for telemetry and optimized LOD management, creating a reusable architecture for future digital twin projects.
