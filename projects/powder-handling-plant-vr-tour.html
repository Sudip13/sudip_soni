Powder Handling Plant VR Tour

RoleUnity Developer & 3D Artist
TechUnity, C#, Blender, VR Controllers, Particle Systems, X-Ray Shaders

Description

An immersive VR walkthrough of a comprehensive powder handling facility, designed to allow users to explore and understand complex industrial processes in a virtual space. The application features a full-scale plant environment where users can freely navigate between different operational stages.

To provide a deeper understanding of the machinery, I implemented a custom "X-Ray Vision" feature that allows users to see through outer casings to visualize internal simulations and mechanical processes in real-time. My core responsibilities included developing optimized 3D models specifically for VR performance, integrating all assets into Unity, and creating a modular architecture using prefabs. I also configured the VR controller setup for seamless navigation and developed advanced particle systems to realistically simulate powder movement and industrial environmental effects.

Outcome

Immersive Process Visualization: Successfully translated complex engineering layouts into an interactive tour, allowing stakeholders to visualize machine simulations and internal processes.

High-Performance VR Execution: Delivered a smooth, high-fidelity experience by utilizing optimized 3D assets and efficient rendering techniques in Unity.

Enhanced Training Capability: Developed a realistic "X-Ray" simulation that provides a unique educational perspective on internal machine functions that are impossible to view in the physical world.

Scalable Development Pipeline: Established a robust workflow for future plant tours by creating a library of industrial prefabs and optimized asset templates.
