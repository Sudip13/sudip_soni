LynxE: 3D Engineering Content Development

Role3D Visualization Specialist & Content Developer
TechBlender, Adobe After Effects, 3D Animation, Technical Visualization

Description

LynxE is an educational initiative focused on simplifying complex engineering principles through high-fidelity 3D animations and visual-first learning modules. My role involved end-to-end production of technical assets designed to translate abstract theoretical concepts into intuitive, observable simulations.

Using Blender, I developed accurate 3D models and animations across topics such as Engineering Drawing, Basic Electronics, and Mechanical Processes. Assets were composed in After Effects with technical call-outs and instructional overlays to ensure clear communication of learning objectives.

Outcome

Technical Concept Visualization: Developed a library of 3D simulations that visualize "invisible" engineering principles, improving clarity of instructional materials.

Professional Animation Pipeline: Established a production workflow from engineering specs to final rendered video content with technical accuracy and visual polish.

Interactive Learning Support: Created visual assets supporting self-paced learning, enabling learners to grasp foundational topics via step-by-step 3D breakdowns.

High-Impact Visual Assets: Produced core technical content for the project’s digital presence, using motion graphics to highlight key data points and process flows.
